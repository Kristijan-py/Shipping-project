Our Story

Brza Pratka is a well-established logistics and delivery company founded in 1998, built on reliability, speed, and continuous growth. Over the years, the company has earned the trust of more than 50,000 clients by delivering consistent, high-quality courier and cargo services. Brza Pratka operates across 100% of North Macedonia, ensuring full national coverage, while also providing cross-border shipping to selected regions in Albania, Kosovo, Serbia, and Bulgaria. With a strong logistics network and experienced team, the company focuses on secure handling, timely deliveries, and customer satisfaction. By combining local expertise with regional reach, Brza Pratka continues to expand its services and strengthen its position as a leading logistics partner in the region.
